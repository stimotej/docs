### Account Registration

To start using Rentiqe, you first need to create an account. Simply visit [rentiqe.com](https://rentiqe.com) and click on
the **“Sign Up”** button. You can also use one-click login with **Google** or **Facebook** for a faster
registration process. Fill in your details and follow the instructions to verify your email.

### Verifying Your Email

After signing up, you will receive a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your
account and start using Rentiqe.

### Logging In

Once your account is verified, return to rentiqe.com and log in with your email and password.
